## Pedagogical structure

The course follows a single transversal case study:

Creciendo Juntos — an integrated early childhood development program.

Across the sessions, participants:

1. Construct a Theory of Change
2. Translate the ToC into indicators
3. Design qualitative investigations
4. Interpret quantitative evidence
5. Assess contribution claims
